Welcome to youth united council of india

Committed to Excellence

Youth United Council of India is a youth-run organization with a central team of passionate and zealous young individuals driven to bring about change. YUCI acts as a unique platform for youth that aids and empowers them through various programs and opportunities. YUCI aims to tackle all issues that hamper the growth and development of young individuals by bringing a wholesome solution. The youth of this council take action to bring change and work towards youth empowerment with their call to action. YUCI is dedicated to strengthen the youth community and help them achieve success in sustainable development. YUCI is not restricted to organising lectures and seminars or activism through social media. We want to empower youth through effective action and aid them to become an important part of the society

Learn More
 

Vision

To empower the youth with positive change and volunteer with social responsibility and create a culture of volunteering for social cause.

Mission

To support the youth to build solid foundation and also to spread social awareness among the society.
